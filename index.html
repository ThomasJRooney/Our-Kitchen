Menu

It’s true, we sell delicious handmade food, quality spirits, and thoughtful specialty groceries & gifts. But our true mission is to spread kindness – to make you feel something, not just buy something. So stop by to grab a chicken sandwich & a smile.

Kindness is a gift everyone can afford to give.

ROSEWATER MARKET

  We serve thoughtfully-crafted food-to-go, from scratch-made baked goods, to breakfast & lunch favorites, to a vast selection of hot and cold drinks. Available year-round at our historic Summer St. storefront.

OUR NAME

The Rosewater name was inspired by the fictional lead character, Eliot Rosewater, from Kurt Vonnegut’s classic “God Bless You Mr. Rosewater” who chose to forgo his family fortune in favor of spreading kindness in his small hometown.

“Hello babies. Welcome to Earth. It’s hot in the summer and cold in the winter. It’s round and wet and crowded. On the outside, babies, you’ve got a hundred years here. There's only one rule that I know of, babies – God damn it, you've got to be kind.”

– Kurt vonnegut –
